Alpaca Login Guide

How to connect your broker account to trade on this platform.

Bring Your Own Key (User Mode)

You can securely connect your own Alpaca account to trade directly from your browser. Your keys are saved locally in your browser and are never sent to our database.

  1. Log in to the Alpaca Dashboard and generate your API Keys.
  2. Create an account or log in to this site.
  3. Click your Profile (or the button below).
  4. Scroll down to the Broker Connection (BYOK) section in the modal.
  5. Paste your Alpaca API Key ID and Alpaca API Secret.
  6. Select Paper Trading or Live Trading from the Trading Mode dropdown, then click Save Profile.
  7. You are now connected! Open the Broker Console to test your connection.
