How To Use
1. Type a ticker and click LOAD TICKER, or pick a scenario and click NEW SESSION
2. Real or synthetic candles reveal one at a time
3. Click LONG or SHORT when you see a setup
4. Advance candles — your P&L updates live
5. Close position when target/stop is hit
6. Drag to move, mousewheel or +/- to zoom, arrows to pan, 0/RST to reset
7. Review your win rate at the end

Goal: Find setups BEFORE seeing the full chart. This trains your pattern recognition.
